Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center! This guide will help you understand and navigate the system efficiently.

Admin Guide

  1. Login

    Use your admin credentials to log in to the system.

  2. Dashboard Overview

    View a summary of tasks, articles, and active members.

  3. Assign Tasks

    Navigate to the "Tasks" section:

    • Click Add Task to assign a new task.
    • Fill in the details such as task title, description, and deadline.
    • Assign the task to specific members or groups.

  4. Review Submitted Articles

    Go to "Submitted Articles" to view and manage article submissions.

  5. Manage Members

    Access the "Members" tab:

    • View a list of all registered members.
    • Update member roles or delete inactive members.

  6. Logout

    Click the logout button in the profile dropdown to exit the system.

Member Guide

  1. Login

    Use your registered username and password to log in.

  2. Access Dashboard

    After login, your dashboard displays assigned tasks and notifications.

  3. View Tasks

    Navigate to the "Tasks" section:

    • View assigned tasks with their deadlines and descriptions.
    • Click on a task to see more details or start working on it.

  4. Submit Tasks

    In the "Tasks" section:

    • Click on the task you want to submit.
    • Upload your completed work and click Submit.

  5. Check Task Status

    Monitor the status of submitted tasks in your dashboard.

  6. Logout

    Click the logout button in the profile dropdown to exit the system.
